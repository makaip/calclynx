Frequently Asked Questions

Is Calclynx really completely free?

Yes! Calclynx is 100% free with no premium features or paywalls. We believe math tools should be accessible to everyone.

What kinds of math can I do with Calclynx?

Calclynx supports a wide range of mathematical operations including algebra, calculus, and more. You can write expressions, equations, and use commands to manipulate them.

Do I need to create an account?

While you can use basic features without an account, creating a free account lets you save your work and access it from any device.

What commands are available in the command palette?

Common commands include factor, simplify, evaluate, solve, expand, and more. Simply start typing the command to see the available options.
